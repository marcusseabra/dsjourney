Ajustando o classpath ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Limpando: 			export CLASSPATH=
Especificando: 	export CLASSPATH=/caminho_jar/nome_jar.jar:/caminho_outro_jar/nome_outro_jar.jar
	Ex.:				export CLASSPATH=/home/seabra/dev/axis/axis-1_4/lib/axis.jar:/home/seabra/dev/axis/axis-1_4/lib/jaxrpc.jar
						export CLASSPATH=/home/seabra/dev/axis/axis-1_4/lib/saaj.jar:/home/seabra/dev/axis/axis-1_4/lib/commons-logging-1.0.4.jar
Visualizando: 		echo $CLASSPATH
